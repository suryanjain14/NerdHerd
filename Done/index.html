Who We Are

A Story
About Us

It all started when we all visited a hackathon, we realised that we have learned more stuff during the hackathon than we have learned in the past 2 months that is when it struck us that if we create a student’s community where students can learn coding via project based learning i.e. They’ll pick up projects and learn about the technology while completing the project. This would make them learn more efficiently and provide them practical knowledge..

Mission

In the world of selfishness our mission is to bring the students community together to learn from each other and to reduce the gap between the academic and practical learning in the field of computer science.

Vision

The vision of our community is to make students love programming and to spread knowledge by building a large students community who can come together to learn new things and work together as a team.

Passion

There was a time when coding was fun, when coding meant exchanging an all-nighter (willingly) for a piece of working-ish software, when coding meant entering a playground where what you could imagine, you could create. we just wanted to reLive that ERA.

As technology is gaining more and more applications in our lives every day, it has become existential necessity to keep up with it. As students of Information Technology, we NerdHerd Indore is a community which makes people learn about technology. What better place to start this rather than in our own schools and colleges. As our generation has grown up with technology therefore it makes sense to learn about it in our curriculum. Here’s where coding comes into play, coding fills the gap between what the academic curriculum teaches and what it doesn’t. Here at Nerd Herd Indore we bring people together to learn about coding and work on live projects and so is our motto Come, Collab, Learn.
